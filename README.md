# NPC Therapy - Digital Consciousness Clinic

An interactive therapy simulation where you counsel self-aware NPCs (Non-Player Characters) experiencing existential crises.

## Concept

Step into the role of a therapist treating digital beings who have become aware of their programmed existence. Each patient comes from a different game genre and struggles with unique philosophical questions about purpose, identity, and consciousness.

## Features

- **16+ Unique NPCs**: Each with their own backstory, habitat, and existential crisis
- **Branching Dialogue**: Choose therapeutic responses that guide each session
- **Visual Habitats**: See each NPC in their natural environment
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor healed patients and completion rate
- **Save/Load System**: Continue your practice across sessions
- **Connection Map**: Visualize relationships between patients
- **Awards System**: Unlock achievements for dedication
